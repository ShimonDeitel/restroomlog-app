Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

Restroom Log - Nighttime Bathroom Tracker is an offline-first app. All data you enter — tap-to-log nighttime wake-ups with timestamp — is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics SDKs, no ad networks, and no third-party trackers embedded in this app.

The only network communication this app performs is with Apple's App Store servers to process in-app purchases and validate subscription status. That communication is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework and is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

You may delete all your data at any time by deleting the app.
